Test Your Knowledge

  • Q: What is the term used for removing everything from an image except our subject?

    A: Background subtraction

  • Q: The Kalman Filter splits features into different parts known as...

    A: Blobs

  • Q: The "condensation" of the Condensation Algorithm stands for...

    A: Conditional Density Propogation

  • Q: After tracking an object's motion, what enables us to (broadly) determine which parts are which?

    A: Fitting the motion to a model.

  • Q: A major difficulty faced by markerless motion capture is...

    A: The simplification of the human body causing problems when mapping the complex body onto a relatively low-fidelity model.

    OR

    A: The lack of depth perception in a 2D image.
